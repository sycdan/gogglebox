# Proof: add-guest flow (eligible tertiary listing, PIN requirement, confirm gating)

## Criterion

The add-guest flow lists eligible tertiary users, requires PIN entry for each
selected guest that needs one, and prevents confirmation until required PINs
are valid.

## How proved

Sandbox stack, `PROOF_FLOW=group-pin` (`e2e/flows/group-pin.mjs`), visitor
account (Carol is a tertiary/guest with pin `5678`), under
`PROOF_RUN_ID=authproof-redo-20260705` (fresh sandbox re-provisioned in this
session's worktree; re-run to get durable evidence after the prior worktree's
`./artifacts` was lost on teardown).

## Evidence (copied into this effort's `.proofs/`, not `./artifacts`)

- Flow step 3 opens the "+ Add guest" modal (`.guest-modal`) and asserts Carol
  is listed there (`guestModal.locator('button.guest-card', { hasText: 'Carol'
  })`), confirming eligible tertiary users are listed. The add-guest modal
  itself is selection-only (no PIN field there by design — PIN is collected
  at Continue time), and the flow explicitly asserts no `input[type=password]`
  exists in that modal.
- `efforts/auth-refactor/.proofs/019f2fc4-08aa-750f-a181-f2f59d041d50.png`
  (orig. `group-pin-prompt.png`) — after clicking "Continue" with Carol
  selected, the continue-time "Enter guest PINs" modal opens showing Carol's
  avatar and a "Carol's PIN" password input, with "Confirm PINs" present. The
  flow asserts this confirm button is DISABLED until a PIN is typed
  (`confirmPinsBtn.isDisabled()` check before screenshotting), i.e. this
  screenshot itself is the disabled-until-input state.
- `efforts/auth-refactor/.proofs/019f2fc4-08aa-783a-b276-4a74853259a1.png`
  (orig. `group-pin-wrong.png`) — an incorrect PIN ("0000") is submitted; the
  server's `POST /api/group/verify-pins` returns 403 and the still-open modal
  shows the error "Incorrect or missing pin for Carol." beneath the PIN
  field — the group is NOT allowed to form and the modal does not close,
  confirming confirmation is prevented until the PIN is valid.
- `efforts/auth-refactor/.proofs/019f2fc4-08ab-7b64-b6dc-e4b75482e1f1.png`
  (orig. `group-pin-success.png`) — retyping the correct PIN (`5678`) in the
  same modal succeeds, the modal closes, and the main app renders —
  confirming a valid PIN unblocks confirmation and group formation.

## Verdict

Proved. Screenshots and flow assertions together show eligible tertiary
listing, PIN requirement at continue-time, and confirm-button gating on PIN
validity (both the disabled-until-typed state and the reject-until-correct
state are visually confirmed).
